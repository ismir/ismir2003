Music IR Conferences







The Lester and Eleanor Levy Family







The fourth international conference on music information retrieval is now over. Thanks to everyone who participated in ISMIR 2003 to make it a success. Copies of the proceedings are available for US $50, payable by Mastercard or Visa. Payment may also be made by check from a US bank or by purchase order. The proceedings will not be shipped until the check has cleared or the purchase order has been paid.

Please contact Toni Decker at tdecker@hd.jhu.edu or (410) 516-3962.



ISMIR 2003 will be co-hosted by the Library of Congress (LC) in Washington, D.C., USA, and Johns Hopkins University(JHU) in Baltimore, Maryland, USA.

The majority of the sessions will take place in Baltimore at the Radisson Lord Baltimore hotel, with events to be held at the Library of Congress as well. It will be co-chaired by Sue Manus (LC) and Sayeed Choudhury (JHU).

Please visit the Accepted Papers and Posters page for the list of acceptances. On that page are instructions for the final submission format.
