From Penn Station (train)
Take St. Paul St. south and make a right onto Lombard St. Make a right onto Howard St. Make a right onto Baltimore Street, and turn right. Follow two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From Points South (Washington, D.C., Richmond, Virginia Beach)
Take I-95 North into Baltimore. Take Exit 53 (I-395 North). I-395 North will become Howard Street. Follow Howard Street to Baltimore Street and turn right. Follow two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From Points North (New York - New Jersey - Philadelphia)
Take I-95 South into Baltimore. Go through the Fort McHenry Tunnel. Take the second exit after the tunnel (Exit 53 - I-395 North). I-395 North will become Howard Street. Follow Howard Street to Baltimore Street and turn right. Follow two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From I-70
Take I-70 to I-695 South (Marked Glen Burnie). Follow I-695 and exit onto I-95 North. Follow I-95 North into Baltimore. Take Exit 53 (I-395 North). I-395 North will become Howard Street. Follow Howard Street to Baltimore Street and turn right. Follow two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From I-83
Take I-83 South to the end and turn right onto Fayette Street. Go nine (9) blocks to Liberty Street and turn left. Make next left onto Baltimore Street and next left at Hanover Street. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From BWI Airport or Downtown Washington, D.C.
Take I-295 North into Baltimore. Once inside the city limits, I-295 will become Russell Street. Once Russell Street crosses Pratt Street it becomes Paca Street. Stay on Paca Street and turn right on Baltimore Street. Follow Baltimore Street two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From Annapolis
Take Route 50 to Route 97. Follow Route 97 to I-695 (towards Towson). Take I-695 to I-295 North (towards Baltimore). Once inside the city limits, I-295 will become Russell Street. Once Russell Street crosses Pratt Street it becomes Paca Street. Stay on Paca Street and turn right on Baltimore Street. Follow Baltimore Street two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From Route 40 East
Route 40 East will become Pulaski Highway. Pulaski Highway will become Orleans Street, and Orleans Street becomes Franklin Street. Once you cross the overpass, follow Franklin Street to Cathedral Street. Cathedral Street will become Liberty Street. Follow Liberty Street to Baltimore Street and make a left onto Baltimore Street. Make the next left onto Hanover Street. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From Route 40 West
Take Route 40 West to Edmondson Avenue. At the cemetery, Edmondson Avenue will become Mulberry Street. Follow Mulberry Street to Cathedral Street. Cathedral Street will become Liberty Street. Follow Liberty Street to Baltimore Street and make a left onto Baltimore Street. Make the next left onto Hanover Street. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From National Airport
Take Route 1 South. Follow Route 1 South until you reach I-495/I-95. Take I-495/I-95 North towards Baltimore. Follow I-95 North to Exit 53 (I-395 North). I-395 North will become Howard Street. Follow Howard Street to Baltimore Street and turn right onto Baltimore Street. Follow Baltimore Street two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left. Hotel entrance is on the right.

From Dulles International Airport
Take the Dulles Access Road East to I-495 North (Washington Beltway). Follow I-495 to I-95 North. Take I-95 North into Baltimore. Take Exit 53 (I-395 North). I-395 North will become Howard Street. Follow Howard Street to Baltimore Street and turn right. Follow two (2) blocks to Hanover Street and turn left.
