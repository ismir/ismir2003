What are the directions to the conference hotel?
You can find driving directions to the hotel here.

What is the registration deadline?
Registration is open until October 22 through the ISMIR website.

What is the location for ISMIR 2003?
The conference sessions will take place at the Radisson Lord Baltimore Hotel, 20 West Baltimore Street, Baltimore Maryland, 21201.

When and where will the conference reception be held?
The conference reception will be held on Monday evening, October 27, 6 p.m., at the Library of Congress in Washington, DC, in the Great Hall.

How will I get to the reception?
Bus transportation will be available at 1:45 p.m. from the Radisson Hotel to the reception and back. Attendees paid $5 for bus transportation to the Library of Congress upon registering for the conference. If you did not pay this fee upon registering but require bus transport, you may pay the $5 when you board the bus.

What meals will be provided during the conference?
Lunch will be provided on Tuesday, October 28. There will also be a banquet dinner on Wednesday, October 29.

What are the hotel phone and fax numbers?
The hotel number is (410) 539-8400. The hotel fax number is (410) 625-1060.
